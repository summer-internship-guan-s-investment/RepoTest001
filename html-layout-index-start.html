About Ourselves:

Fabulous J&F Jewelry repair has been in the jewelry business since 2000. Specializing in design, manufacturing, and the repair of jewellery with various precious metals and gems. Over the years, our qualified staff and goldsmiths have served and continued to provide realistic service to meet our customer’s needs.

Our traditional hand-stitched technique has won the respect of not only our customers, but they also earned the respect of our competitors in the industry.

Our metal crafting with the dragonfly and Milgrain technique is well know in the industry and praised by our customers. We strive to preserve customers value in our work and products that will endure that the test of time.
